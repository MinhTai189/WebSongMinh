Initially established in 2020 by a group of lecturers and researchers of the Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, SongMinh is a young company and represents the synergy between knowledge acquired in academic research activities and years of experience in developing applied solutions. The company has now readily offered technical consulting services, especially in the transport-and construction-related field.

Our company was born in the most dynamic and active region in Vietnam, where both the economy and traffic have grown fast.
